# Intelligence Layer

AI/OCR extraction and bank reconciliation intelligence are outside the active Version 1 scope.

Bank reconciliation and official accounting records are maintained in SQL Accounting. This application supports expense administration, payment preparation and supporting-document control.

## Active Rule-Based Assistance

- Missing supporting-document status.
- Three-day due reminders for supplier bills.
- Recurring-obligation draft generation when scheduling fields are complete.
- Payment voucher numbering at issue time.

## Disabled Or Future-Only

- OCR invoice extraction
- AI invoice parsing
- Bank statement matching
- Bank reconciliation confidence scoring
- Automatic accounting ledger posting
